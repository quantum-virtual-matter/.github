# Quantum Virtual Matter

Welcome to **Quantum Virtual Matter**.

We are building at the intersection of **quantum science, simulation, and virtual matter systems**. Our work explores how complex physical behavior can be modeled, understood, and extended through computational tools, interactive research workflows, and open collaboration.

## What we do

- Develop ideas and tools inspired by quantum materials and emergent matter
- Explore simulation-driven approaches to understanding complex systems
- Support research, experimentation, and reproducible computational workflows
- Share projects openly so others can learn from, build on, and contribute to them

## Why this organization exists

Quantum Virtual Matter is a home for projects that investigate how matter, information, and computation connect. We aim to create a space for thoughtful experimentation across physics, software, and scientific infrastructure.

## Get involved

We welcome collaborators, researchers, and curious builders.

You can get involved by:
- Exploring our repositories
- Opening issues for bugs, ideas, or questions
- Contributing improvements through pull requests
- Following project documentation as it becomes available

## Resources

- Browse the repositories in this organization to see active work
- Check individual project READMEs for setup instructions and contribution details
- Use Discussions or Issues where available to ask questions and share ideas

## Vision

Our long-term goal is to support open, rigorous, and imaginative work around simulated and computationally explored matter systems.
